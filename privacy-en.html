Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is the policy of Trilha DevOps Academy Workshop to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect on this site.

1. Data Collection

We request personal information (such as name and email) only when we genuinely need it to provide you with access to the workshop, learning materials or important communications about the event.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Secure your registration and access to the classes;
  • Send streaming links and supporting materials;
  • Inform you about future sessions or relevant DevOps content.

3. Data Retention

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide the requested service. When we store data, we protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorised access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

4. Sharing

We do not share personally identifiable information publicly or with third parties, except when required by law or for email automation platforms necessary for content delivery.

5. Your Rights

You are free to decline our request for personal information, understanding that we may be unable to provide some of the desired services. Your continued use of our site will be considered acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, please contact us through the workshop support email.
